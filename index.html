Welcome to Vardan Academy

Nestled in the greenery of Mahona (U.P, India), our school provides a comprehensive educational journey from kindergarten through senior secondary school. Renowned for its rigorous academic standards, we consistently excel in both scholastic competitions and sporting events. Our students benefit from state-of-the-art facilities, including well-equipped laboratories, modern classrooms, an extensive library, and dedicated computer labs fostering digital literacy from an early age. This nurturing environment promotes holistic growth and achievement in all spheres.

    Our salient features:
  • CBSE Board approved
  • Computer classes for all students
  • Annual School Sports
  • Competitions

School Sports

Our school is a hub of vibrant sporting activity, fostering teamwork, discipline, and athletic achievement across various disciplines.

Competitions

Our school actively participates and excels in a wide array of academic and extracurricular competitions, fostering a spirit of healthy rivalry and achievement.

Facilities we offer

Our commitment to holistic education is reflected in our modern infrastructure, which includes well-equipped chemistry and biology laboratories for hands-on scientific exploration, dedicated computer labs fostering digital literacy, and bright, spacious classrooms conducive to effective learning. These facilities provide students with an enriching environment to excel academically and develop essential skills for the future.
